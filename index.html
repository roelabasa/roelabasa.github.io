My Story

I'm Roel born in Cebu but raised in Glan Saranggani Province. I graduated in CITE Technical Institute Inc, taking up Information Technology last May 2003. I had my first job experience in the last 3 semesters of my studies. This is due to the training scheme adopted by CITE which comprises of an in-plant and in-school training. So it's definitely keeping the students to be job oriented. This training scheme is called Dual-Training-System (DTS). The students will render a 5-day work in the company and will report to the school for one day since there are still academic subjects taken beside the DTS. In my case, I was placed in Lexmark Research and Development Corporation where I was assigned as Macintosh OS Printer Driver tester.

I shifted my career to being a programmer, when I got hired as a Multimedia Programmer and eventually, inclined into software development for about 10 years now.
